Our Story

Bringing True Thailand
to Chennai's Heart

Benjarong — named after the five-coloured royal Thai porcelain — stands as Chennai's most celebrated Thai restaurant. Nestled in the cultural heart of Alwarpet, we have been serving dishes that honour Thailand's royal culinary heritage with uncompromising authenticity.

From the first spoonful of our aromatic Green Curry to the last bite of our legendary Pad Thai, every plate is an invitation to travel — without leaving your seat.

The Experience

More Than a Meal —
An Immersion

"Excellent location, ambience and high quality cuisine and service" — these are the words our guests use. Step into a world of warm teakwood tones, flickering candlelight, and the gentle scent of lemongrass. Benjarong is where special occasions are made unforgettable.

Located on iconic TTK Road in Alwarpet, we are Chennai's destination for authentic Thai dining — a haven for food lovers, a favourite for celebrations.
